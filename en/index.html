This blog is dedicated on tabletop roleplaying games in theory and practice.

Our journey will be documented in different forms, essay, ttrpg reviews (mostly russian) and other original content.

On this journey we will move to different places — from game mechanics to narrative, from well-known games to hidden indie gems, and finally, from theory of ttrpg to its practical application.

I hope that all of this will lead us to better understanding of tabletop RPG
